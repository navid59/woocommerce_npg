Welcome to NETOPIA Payments system

NETOPIA Payments Woocommerce Payment Gateway extends WooCommerce payment options by adding NETOPIA’s Payment Gateway options.

Features:
  • - 100% FREE TO USE (GPLv2 license).
  • - Integrates NETOPIA payments’ card and cryptocoin payments service with your WordPress + WooCommerce online shop. SMS and Wire transfer options are still under development.
  • - Accepts payments with Visa and Mastercard credit/debit cards, Bitcoin and Ethereum
  • - Handles IPN responses and automatically changes order status on your shop in real time (confirmed/paid or failure messages and refunds).
Easy steps to LIVE payments:
  • - Firstly, chose NETOPIA Payments Setting tab and set the configurations.
  • - By getting help from NETOPIA Payments Agreements tab set and testing the necessary conditions.
  • - Save your setting.
  • - Any time you ready to connect your website to LIVE Payment, just click on Ask for Go Live Verification in NETOPIA Payments Agreements tab.
  • - By Clicking on Ask for Go Live Verification we receive your request & will back to you soon
Important NOTE:

NOTE #1: the Ask for Go Live Verification button is only for LIVE mode. During your implementation or your testing period you don't need to click on this button

NOTE #2: is important to asking for reverify by NETOPIA Payments support After any changes in your Payment settings
